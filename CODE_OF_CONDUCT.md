# Code of Conduct

Project participation must remain professional, technical, and respectful.

## Expected conduct

- Discuss code and evidence rather than attacking people.
- Respect privacy and never post credentials, private logs, or personal data.
- Give clear reproduction steps and accept constructive review.
- Credit prior work and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, spam, or deliberately disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content, close abusive discussions, restrict participation, or report serious conduct to the relevant platform. Private conduct concerns may be sent to `hello@vib.tools`.
